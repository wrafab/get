Week 6 - Lamination, Vacuum Forming, Molding & Casting

First project this week is making a skateboard. First, we put seven boards on top of each other. The patterns of the board can be either horizontal, making the wood hard, or vertical, making it soft. We stack the boards in the order: two horizontal, vertical, horizontal, vertical, two horizontal. Put glue between boards. The boards do not have to line up perfectly now. Put the glued skateboard between two bigger, heavier baords. Put several clamps so that there is enough pressure to make the skateboard kept in shape. Wait for a day to let the glue dry. After glue dried, use this machine to flatten edge of skateboard. Use this machine to smooth the edges. Use this machine to flatten two ends of skateboard. Decorate the skateboard with spray paint, laser engraving, or stickers. I meant to put a green 'WRA' on top of white background, but I did not let the background dry first and messed up.
